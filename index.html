Postage and Returns Policy

Shipping

We deliver parcels world wide all the time. Costs vary depending on countries and the weight of the parcel being sent. Also note the price quoted is for Shipping Only and does not include any fees or government taxes/treatment of product associated in accordance to your Customs and Quarantine regulations. Feel free to put through your order and, if you are not happy with the postage costs, let us know and we can cancel the order for you. Otherwise, just ask us for a quote.

Returns

If you don’t want it, send it back to us within 90 days of ordering and we’ll happily refund the value of the goods. Postage charges will not be refunded on unwanted items. To be eligible for a refund the goods must be unopened or unused in any way at all. We must be able to resell the goods for full value once returned to us. If returns arrive in poor or unsalable condition the refund will not be available. You are responsible for ensuring the goods are returned to us. Dasmoto's Arts & Crafts will not be liable for returns that are lost in the post or lost for any other reason.
